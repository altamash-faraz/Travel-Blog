Frequently Asked Questions

How do you choose your destinations?

I love discovering both popular and off-the-beaten-path destinations. Sometimes it's spontaneous, sometimes it's months of planning based on recommendations from fellow travelers!

Can I use your photos?

Please reach out before using any photos. I'm usually happy to share with proper attribution for non-commercial use.

Do you accept collaborations?

I'm open to collaborations that align with my values of responsible and authentic travel. Feel free to reach out with your proposal!

Do you provide travel planning services?

While I don't offer formal planning services, I'm always happy to share tips and recommendations for destinations I've visited!
